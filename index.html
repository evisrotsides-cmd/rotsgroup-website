Our Story

At Rots Group, we began with a single property and a simple goal — to create spaces that inspire, comfort, and stand the test of time. What started as a passion for thoughtful design and hospitality soon evolved into a company known for transforming properties into meaningful experiences.

From those humble beginnings, we’ve grown steadily — guided by a clear vision, meticulous attention to detail, and an unshakeable commitment to quality. Every project we take on, whether a boutique hotel, a serviced apartment, or a private residence, is treated as an opportunity to blend functionality with timeless aesthetics.

Our success is rooted in the belief that true value lies in more than just structures — it’s about the people who experience them. We focus on creating environments that enhance everyday living while delivering sustainable financial performance for our partners and investors.

Today, Rots Group stands as a bridge between design and business, merging hospitality insight with property strategy. With operations across Europe and Africa, we continue to expand our footprint, collaborating with visionaries who share our drive for excellence.

We remain, at our core, inspired by the same principles that shaped our beginning: passion, precision, and purpose. Every space we touch reflects our enduring philosophy — that extraordinary places are not simply built; they are crafted with care, intention, and heart.
